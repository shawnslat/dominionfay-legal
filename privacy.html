Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Dominion Christian Center mobile application (“the App”), operated by Dominion Christian Center Fayetteville, located in Fayetteville, North Carolina.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (last 5 digits only, securely hashed)

Children’s Information (For Check-In Safety Only)

  • Child’s name
  • Child’s age

Children’s data is used strictly for in-church check-in and safety purposes. Children cannot create accounts.

Prayer Requests

Prayer requests submitted within the App may be visible to other app members through the community prayer wall.

Push Notification Tokens

We collect device push notification tokens solely to send service reminders, announcements, and church updates.

Voluntary Giving Records

Giving records are stored locally within the app for personal tracking and reference. We do not store or process payment information within the App.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide church communication and ministry updates
  • To support child check-in and safety procedures
  • To display prayer requests within the church community
  • To send push notifications (which may be disabled at any time)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Information is accessible only to authorized church leadership and staff for operational purposes.

Push Notifications

Push notifications may be disabled at any time within the App or through your device settings.

Data Deletion

You may delete your account and all associated data directly within the App at any time.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information, including hashing sensitive identifiers and secure authentication.

Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of North Carolina.
