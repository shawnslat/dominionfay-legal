Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

Vibecode (“the App”) is operated by Dominion Christian Center. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information responsibly.

Information We Collect

The App collects only the information necessary to support church-related functionality.

Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)

Family & Nursery Information

Entered by parents or legal guardians:

  • Child name
  • Allergies or special care notes
  • Nursery check-in and check-out records
  • Authorized pickup details

Church Participation

  • Event registrations
  • Prayer requests (public or private, based on user selection)
  • Devotional interactions and sermon notes

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Manage church membership features
  • Support nursery check-in and child safety
  • Facilitate church events and communications
  • Display devotional and sermon-related content

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or tracking.

Data Sharing

Your information is not sold or shared with third parties.

Access is limited to:

  • You (the account holder)
  • Authorized church staff for operational purposes only

Children’s Privacy

Information about children is entered and managed only by parents or legal guardians for nursery and family ministry purposes. The App does not allow children to create accounts.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including authentication, access controls, and database row-level security to protect your information.

Data Retention & Deletion

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting church leadership or app support.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be reflected within the App.
